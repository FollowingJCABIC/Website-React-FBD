# The First Light
I woke up before the sun and tried to listen to the room instead of rushing it.

> If I can hear the quiet, I can answer it.

## What I am keeping
- Three people to pray for by name.
- One small act of service for today.
- A reminder that **silence** is still a way of speaking.
